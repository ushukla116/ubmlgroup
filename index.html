Deep Fakes Detection through Action Units

Deepfakes are synthetic media which involves changing a person’s identity in an image or a video by replacing them with someone’s else likeliness. Generally, deepfakes are generated using Autoencoders or Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs). The detection of deepfakes involves using Machine Learning and Artificial intelligence techniques to detect whether an image or a video is fake or not.
